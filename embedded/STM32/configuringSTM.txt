Here's the brief CubeMX configuration for your STM32H723VGT6 with 4x4 MEMS mic array:
CubeMX Configuration:

1. I2S Setup:
Enable I2S2 or I2S3
Mode: Master Receiver
Standard: I2S Philips
Data Format: 16-bit
Audio Frequency: 72kHz

2. DMA Setup:
Enable DMA1 Stream 3 (for I2S2_RX)
Mode: Circular
Data Width: Half Word (16-bit)
Priority: High
Enable Half Transfer Complete and Transfer Complete interrupts

3. GPIO Pins:
PC2: I2S2_CK (Bit Clock)
PC3: I2S2_SD (Serial Data)
PA4: I2S2_WS (Word Select)

4. Clock Configuration:
Enable PLLI2S for I2S clock generation
Set I2S clock source to PLLI2S
